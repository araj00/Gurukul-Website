About Us

SHREE SWAMINARAYAN VISHVAMANGAL GURUKUL, KALOL (SSVG) a Hindu Vedic Monasterywas established in 1992. To form the charitable trust was a dream of Managing Trustee- Shastri Swami Shree Premswarupdasji, who wanted to do something for the betterment of society and materialized his dream by providing his huge share of his property for this noble cause. The trust is fully devoted to the noble cause of spreading quality education, provide best health care facility and provide huge employment opportunities in the region. Shree Swaminarayan Vishvamangal Gurukul is a dream come tru, a dream nursed by Shastri Swami Shree Premswarudasji to have a centre of excellence in learning for the benefit of all. With headquarters at Kalol and campuses at Ahmedabad in Gujarat, Bhinmal and Jalore in Rajasthan. Shree Swaminarayan Vishvamangal Gurukul as emerged as one of the fastest growing institutions of higher learning in India.

In less than two decades, Shree Swaminarayan Vishvamangal Gurukul has blossomed into a multi-disciplinary, multicampus centre that offer over 50+ diploma, undergraduate and postgraduate programmes. Currently, SSVG trust more than 10000 students studying in various educational courses in field of Medical (B.A.M.S., B.H.M.S.), Paramedical (Nursing, Physiotherapy,Pharmacy, Medical Lab Technology) , Engineering, other under graduate and post graduate courses which are approved by competent government authorities and affiliated with their respective university. SSVG also run CBSE and GSEB approved, primary to higher secondary schools in English and Gujarati medium. The trust disburses several scholarships every year to the poor and meritorious students, enabling more and more underprivileged people of taste the fruits of education. Beside education the trust also managies Allopathic, Ayurvedic and Homoeopathic Super Specialty Hospitals. The Allopathic Hospital is empanelled with government schemes like PM-JAY, MAA Amrutam Yojana etc. where Ayurvedic and Homoeopathic Hospital provides free diagnostic facility.

Shree Swaminarayan Vishvamangal Gurukul draws its vision, inspiration and strength from the founder Shastri Swami Premswaroopdasji. Swamijij has dedicated his life as religious and spiritual Saint voluntarily and spending his life with an intention to make people free from evil clutches of negative influence of present era by maintaining the gesture of Sacrament in Society. He started his journey as a saint at very early age of life in the Swaminarayan Mandir located in Bhuj where he had spent 17 Years. Swamiji says a Gujarati proverb “Sadhu to Chalta Bhala” which is implemented in his whole journey of life. In 1985, Swamiji left Bhuj Swaminarayan Temple and arrived to Swaminarayan Temple, Kalupur. After serving few years there Swamiji did Mahantai at Shree Swaminarayan Temple, Narayanpura-Ahmedabad where he arranged Satsang Khathas in the surrounding towns and villages which has given enlightenment to many followers. By the humble order of Acharya Maharajshri, Swamiji moved to Shree Swaminarayan Mandir, Siddhpur. After development of Siddhpur Temple Swamiji came to Kalol, an entrance of North Gujarat Region and acquired a piece of barren land and dreamed to establish a gurukul providing education, shelter to helpless peoples and a Gaushala. The first foundation stone was laid down by Param Pujya Dharm Dhurandhar 1008 Acharya Shri Tejendraprasadji in the year 1992. Thereafter, Swamiji continued Satsang, Kathas and religious meetings in the nearby Towns and Villages. Then initially started giving education to few students. Swamiji had started a School, then gradually he established various educational institutes like B.Ed., PTC, Pharmacy, Diploma-Degree-Masters Engineering in Kalol campus. Then he established another branch of SSVG in Ranip-Ahmedabad where International School and Physiotherapy institute were started. To provide quality health care facility Swamiji has started hospital in Kalol campus followed that Medical and Paramedical courses have been started. Moreover, through Swamiji’s vision Swaminarayan University came into existence from 1st June, 2022. Swamiji strongly believes that an institution’s true strength lies in the hearts and souls of its teachers and students who come together with a purpose and firm determination to exchange the knowledge and let the whole society be the beneficiary.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote participation in higher education and the Strategic Plan assesses our progress and looks to the future. The investment on-campus development plan is a benefit to our students, staff and the local community. The Trust policies and regulations ensure we maintain high standards. We will pursue continuous development of infrastructure and enhance state-of-the-art equipment to provide our students a technologically up-to-date and intellectually inspiring environment of learning, research, creativity, innovation and professional activity and inculcate in them ethical and moral values.

Our Vision

To emerge as a “Centre for excellence” offering Education and Research Opportunities of very high standards to students, develop the total personality of the individual, and install high levels of discipline and strive to set global standards, making our students technologically superior and ethically strong, who in turn shall contribute to the advancement of society and humankind. We dedicate and commit ourselves to achieve, sustain and foster unmatched excellence in Education.
